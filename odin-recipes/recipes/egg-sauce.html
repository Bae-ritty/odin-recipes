Egg sauce

EGG SAUCE

home

Description

Egg sauce is a simple, flavorful dish made by cooking eggs together with tomatoes, onions, and peppers. The vegetables are first fried to create a rich, tasty base, and then beaten eggs are added to form a soft, savory mixture. The result is a colorful sauce that is both comforting and satisfying.

This dish is popular because it is quick to prepare and pairs well with many foods. It can be served with yam, bread, rice, or potatoes, making it a versatile option for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Its warm, rich flavor and soft texture make egg sauce a favorite in many households.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 2 Tomatoes(chopped)
  • 1 Onion(chopped)
  • 1-2 pepper
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cube
  • oil

Steps

  1. Heat a little oil in a pan and fry the chopped onions until soft.-
  2. Add the tomatoes and pepper, then cook until the tomatoes reduce and thicken.
  3. Add salt and seasoning cube, then stir well.
  4. Beat the eggs in a bowl and pour them into the tomato mixture.
  5. Let it cook for a few seconds, then gently stir until the eggs are fully set.
  6. Serve with yam, bread, rice, or anything you like.
