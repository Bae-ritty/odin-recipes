JELLOF RICE

home

Description

Jollof rice is a famous West African dish made by cooking rice in a flavorful tomato and pepper sauce. The rice absorbs the spices and seasonings, giving it a rich taste and a bright red color.

It is often served with chicken, beef, or plantains and is enjoyed at parties and family gatherings. Jollof rice is loved for its bold flavor and warm, comforting taste.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato |(fresh or canned)
  • Onion
  • Red ball pepper
  • Scotch bonnet
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cube
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaves

optional

  • Chicken, beef or fish
  • Butter
  • Vegetables (peas, carrots)

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions into a smooth mixture.
  2. Heat oil, fry onions, then add tomato paste and blended mix.
  3. Add seasoning, curry, thyme, and stock; cook the sauce.
  4. Pour in washed rice and mix well.
  5. Add water or stock, cover, and cook on low heat.
  6. Stir gently and let it finish steaming until soft and fluffy.
